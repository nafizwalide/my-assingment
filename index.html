Cause Of This Flood

From 11 May 2022, heavy rains and mountain slopes continue. Due to this flood started in Sylhet. The Surma, Sari, the Luva, the Dhalai, Kushiyara flows through the interior of the district, and the water flows through the danger zone at each point. As the upstream water continues to recede, the lowlands are inundated anew.[11] According to 16 June, the flood situation worsened in the Sylhet region due to the rising river Surma and Kushiyara.[12] According to experts, the river navigability crisis and the construction of unplanned dams in the lakes are responsible for the flood situation.
